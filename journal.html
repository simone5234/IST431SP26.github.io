Journal

Questions used for interview and journal section

I used a combination of some of the prompt questions as well as my own, and gave them to two people that I know who play Roblox to get their input on the overall themes of the project. I haven't changed their responses as shown below, and they're separated by color.

What do you use Roblox for?(ex. play games, hangout with friends, customization, game development)

I mostly play only play Roblox with my little brother (what ever he wants to play). Every once in a while ill play random incremental games since I love mindlessly clicking buttons and watching numbers go up.

Playing free games with friends

Who uses Roblox around you?(ex. my best friend and I; my sisters and brothers, etc)

My little cousins and little brothers use Roblox.

Friends, Brother, and Sister

Why did you decide to use Roblox?

My little brother wanted me to play it with him, so I installed it, although I had played it when i myself was a child but stopped around my late teens when most of my friends dropped it.

It is a free platform as well as the ease of accessibility

Do you notice any safety concerns with Roblox? (Can be based on previous experiences using it or general knowledge)

A huge safety concern that I've noticed and experienced is that a lot of the games targeted towards children have a huge incentive to make them want to gamble for in-game cosmetics/items using real life currency, and I believe that Roblox does nothing to stop this and is mostly okay with the gambling targeted to children.

Absolutely, with the age of the average user being so low the platform attracts many people with negative intentions

Does Roblox do its job as a gaming platform well?

I would say yes they do a good job at promoting new games while also catering to what is interesting to the player.

To an extent, as a gaming platform I believe they provide many tools to developers allowing them to create a wide variety of different experiences. As a social platform I believe that Roblox has shown itself to be far too lenient with their moderation decisions.

How do you feel about being able to create a digital identity through Roblox? Is it something that is important for you when it comes to gaming platforms?(Can compare it to Steam or Minecraft)

For me, it's not a huge thing since I casually play Roblox but for the average I feel like it's way easier to create your own identity compared to other games like Minecraft where you're limited on what your player can have equipped via cosmetics. Roblox offer way more freedom for one to express themselves.

The level of customization allowed through Roblox is a massive part of why I enjoy the platform. I myself find a lot of value in the extensive customization.

What's something that Roblox does that can't be compared to other gaming platforms?(If there is no solid answer that's ok)

They give players the tools to be able to create their own games and I think this is a huge thing that can't compare to other platforms since it makes it easy for kids to learn how to code and create things for free.

With Roblox I enjoy the lack of commitment necessary to many of their games. I can play something for 5 minutes and be satisfied, or I can play for a few hours and dedicate a significant amount of time.
